Consulting

23 July 2023 · 2 min · 333 words · KshitijAgrawal

Having seen multiple 0-to-1 journies, I enjoy the challenge of building and scaling new technological systems. I have worked across marketplaces, chipmakers, military-tech, gaming/adtech, generative apps, hrms systems and many more. Here is how I like to select projects

  • High-Value Projects: I seek projects that leverage my expertise and create significant impact for founders, and in return, equitable compensation.
  • Exploring new industries: I intend to pursue opportunities that explore different avenues and industries while leveraging my knowledge across systems.
  • Giving back: Community efforts like speaking , mentoring and judging at events

Advisor vs. Consulting

  • Consulting: I operate on a retainer-based model with a project plan that we jointly identify and agree upon. I typically pick projects with a major AI component, often augmented with resources from your internal team.

  • Advisor: I dedicate a day or half a day per month to provide guidance and support to startups and founders at various stages of their AI journey.

Consulting Process#

Situation Assessment - I try to assess your actual needs based on discussions with you and your team. Our conversations will focus on:

  • Objectives - Business/Product Objectives
  • Metrics - Product/Model Metrics
  • Benefits - What does it unlock for your business

By following this approach, I try to understand the root of what we need to do, how I can bring my expertise to the table, and how we measure success, and quantify the impact of the work.

Here are a sample of my projects

  • I’m a founder of a GenerativeAI app. We have been building a computer vision pipeline from the last 2 years. Now we need help to scale this as our user base has grown significantly and we need the best possible performance.

  • I’m a technical founder working on my second startup, and I need someone who brings ML expertise to help us develop a roadmap, hire and execute.

  • I’m a VC and I’d love to get your thoughts on “some-ai-technology” and help with some due diligence.
